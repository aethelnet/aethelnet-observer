# How to Use Market Connections Network

**Quick guide to operating the visualization on port 1421**

---

## 🎯 **What You're Looking At**

This is a **3D network graph** showing how trading markets are connected:

- **Colored Spheres (Nodes)** = Trading markets (BTCEUR, ETHEUR, etc.)
  - 🟢 **Green** = Buy signal
  - 🔴 **Red** = Sell signal  
  - ⚪ **Gray** = Neutral
  - **Size** = Trading volume (bigger = more volume)

- **Lines (Connections)** = Market correlations
  - **Thicker lines** = Stronger correlation
  - **Color** = Correlation strength (Green=Strong, Yellow=Medium, Gray=Weak)

---

## 🖱️ **Basic Controls**

### **Mouse Controls:**
1. **Click and Drag** = Rotate the 3D view
2. **Scroll Wheel** = Zoom in/out
3. **Click a Node** = Select it and see its connections

### **Buttons (Bottom Left):**
- **Reset View** = Return camera to default position
- **Toggle Connections** = Show/hide all connection lines
- **Toggle Labels** = Show/hide correlation labels
- **Reset Filter** = Reset correlation filter to 30%

---

## 📊 **How to Explore**

### **Step 1: Find the Nodes**
- Look for **colored spheres** in the 3D space
- They should be visible as glowing spheres
- If you don't see them, try **scrolling to zoom in**

### **Step 2: Click a Node**
- Click any colored sphere
- It will **glow brighter** when selected
- The **details panel** appears in the left sidebar
- **Only connections to that node** are highlighted

### **Step 3: Filter Connections**
- Use the **slider** in the left panel
- Move it right to show only **strong correlations** (e.g., 70%+)
- Move it left to show **weaker connections** too

### **Step 4: Toggle Labels**
- Click **"Toggle Labels"** button
- Labels show **symbol names + correlation %** at line endpoints
- Useful for seeing exact correlation values

---

## 🔍 **Troubleshooting**

### **Can't See the Nodes?**
1. **Scroll to zoom in** - They might be too far away
2. **Click "Reset View"** - Returns to default camera position
3. **Rotate the view** - Drag to see from different angles
4. Nodes are **3-8 units in size** - They should be visible

### **Can't Click Nodes?**
- Make sure you're clicking on the **colored spheres**, not the lines
- Try **zooming in** first to make them easier to click
- Nodes are clickable anywhere on the sphere

### **Labels Not Showing?**
- Click **"Toggle Labels"** button
- Labels appear at the **ends of connection lines**
- They show symbol names and correlation percentages

---

## 💡 **Tips**

- **Start by zooming in** to see nodes clearly
- **Click different nodes** to explore connections
- **Use the filter slider** to focus on strong correlations
- **Rotate the view** to see the network from all angles
- **Selected nodes glow brighter** - easy to spot

---

## 🎨 **What the Colors Mean**

### **Node Colors:**
- 🟢 **Green** = Algorithm says "BUY"
- 🔴 **Red** = Algorithm says "SELL"
- ⚪ **Gray** = No clear signal

### **Line Colors:**
- 🟢 **Green lines** = Strong correlation (70%+)
- 🟡 **Yellow lines** = Medium correlation (50-70%)
- ⚪ **Gray lines** = Weak correlation (30-50%)
